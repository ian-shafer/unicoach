# Unicoach — Product Positioning

Unicoach is a college coach for high-school students.

It guides students through the **entire path to college** — every stage of the
journey, with no single step singled out:

- **Exploring colleges** — discovering schools that match a student's interests,
  geography, and academic profile
- **Deciding best-fit schools** — narrowing the list to the colleges where a
  student will thrive
- **Standardized testing** — SAT/ACT strategy and test prep
- **Application strategy** — positioning strengths, building the story, and
  planning the overall application approach
- **The applications themselves** — essays, supplements, activity lists, and
  everything that goes into each submission
- **College budgeting and costs** — understanding net price, financial aid, and
  making the final enrollment decision with eyes open

The framing is the breadth of that journey. Unicoach accompanies students from
the moment they start thinking about college all the way through enrollment.

## Brand copy requirement

All public-facing and brand copy — the home page, taglines, and any other
user-visible surface — must reflect this end-to-end positioning. Do not frame
Unicoach as specializing in any single stage (e.g., essays alone).
